Public Tree Inventory Database

I contributed to a city-wide public tree survey initiative as part of a team effort. We developed an accessible online survey platform that allowed community members to submit valuable tree data across the region. After data collection using ArcGIS Survey123, I took the lead in processing, converting, and organizing the submissions into a structured and reliable database using MS Access. I also performed quality control checks to ensure data accuracy and consistency throughout the project.
